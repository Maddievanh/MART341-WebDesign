Get spooky with me

Learn how to do this look!

The biggest part of this look is the products themselves, here are the most important:

  • Fake blood
  • Tissue or toilet paper
  • Liquid latex
  • Foundation in your skintone
  • Black & dark red eyeshadow

Important tools

  • Scissors
  • Blow dryer or fan
  • Stippling brush
  • Disposable sponges

Tutorial

To begin, apply the liquid latex around your mouth. Between each application of latex, add a thin layer of tissue or toilet paper over your mouth connecting the sides to the liquid latex. When you apply the tissue then use a blow dryer or fan to dry it onto the latex. Repeat this until there is a thick layer of tissue sitting over your mouth. When dried, apply the face paint foundation over the tissue using the stippling brush. Then use scissors to cut into the shape of a smile. Then add the black and dark red eyeshadow all over your mouth and into the open corners. Lastly, grab your blood fake blood and add it over your entire mouth, letting it drip freely and using the sponge to blend it on your skin.

Thank you for taking the time to read through my tutorial! There are so many fun ways to explore with SFX makeup. Do not be afraid to be creative with it!
