Maddies Eats


Desparado located in Missoula, Montana. I reside in Missoula, and this is one of my top places to go to. It is your perfect sports bar with the best food. Before the burger, get the wings!

VanHorns in Media, Pennsylviana. This is my dads restaurant, and the most special. There is nothing better than a cheesesteak and sloppy fries. There is no doubt they make the best.

1889 is another favorite of mine. Located in Missoula, Montana this is your best spot for a juicy steak. The "Sophia Lauren" is their top drink, as it should be! Delicious food, great service.
